# Microsoft Excel

How to get beginning of month in excel? #card 
`=date-DAY(date)+1`

How to get beginning of week in excel? #card 
`=A2-WEEKDAY(A2,2)+1`

How to get beginning of year in excel? #card 
`=DATE(year,1,1)`
